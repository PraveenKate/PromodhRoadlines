Our Services

Our Main Focus

Reliable Parcel Delivery & Special Vehicle

Our special vehicle services cater to shipments needing specific handling, such as oversized, fragile, or temperature-sensitive goods. We provide specialized vehicles like refrigerated trucks or flatbeds, tailored to your shipment's needs.

Door Delivery&Collection

We offer door delivery and door collection services for maximum convenience and ease. With door delivery, goods are transported directly to the recipient’s doorstep, while door collection allows us to pick up shipments from your location. Both services are seamless, reliable, and supported by real-time tracking for hassle-free logistics.

Part Load & Full Load

We offer full load and part load services to meet your transportation needs. Full load ensures exclusive, secure, and direct delivery for large shipments, while part load provides a cost-effective option for smaller consignments by sharing vehicle space.

Godown & Special Delivery

We provide godown delivery for seamless transport to warehouses, ensuring secure unloading and efficient inventory management, and special delivery for urgent, time-sensitive shipments with priority handling and real-time updates to meet tight deadlines.
